Money Fellows is the evolution of Money Circles, it’s:

  • Always available at a click of a button.
  • Payout values that reach 120K EGP.
  • Installments start as low as 250 EGP/month
  • Ability to join multiple Money Circles at the same time.
  • Not bound by your current social circle or geographical location.
  • Private. Nobody knows when you are getting your payout.
  • Secured. Everyone signs a legally binding contract.
  • Various Pay-in and Payout methods.

  • Money Circles values start from 3,000 EGP and go all the way up to 120,000 EGP.
  • Installments start from as low as 250 EGP per month and up to 20,000 EGP per month.
  • You can choose between 3 different durations; 6 Months, 10 Months, and 12 Months.

Each user has to sign a legally binding contract after going through our credit assessment to make sure that all users can pay their installments. In case other users fail to pay their due amounts, Money Fellows covers their share to make sure you get your full payout.
